About

Overview

Project Feed 1010's software service aims to provide aquaponics system maintainers with a means to capture and store information about their systems. Storing data on our service enables them to monitor their system on the go and share experience with other maintainers in our network.

Measurement Storage

We have automated the submission of measurement results (currently for Vernier probeware products, please contact us if you are interested in support for probeware from other vendors). Alternatively, maintainers can either enter their measurement information manually or import it through the web application.

Monitoring & Analytics

Maintainers can use their measurement information in various ways: realtime information can be utilised for automated monitoring and wearing notifications, while historic information and meta data can be used for analysis.

Become Part of a Community

By signing up for this service (requires a Google login), maintainers will join a network of maintainers worldwide and share experiences to help one another improving their Aquaponics systems.

Support for Mobile Devices

Download our mobile app to receive alerts from your Aquaponics system on the go.
